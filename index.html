Categories Plot:






















For always voters, it seems pretty evenly split between education levels, with maybe slightly more of them having college education; they also look evenly split between income levels. Sporadic voters also look evenly split amongst again with slightly more being college educated; income levels look evenly split. Finally, the rarely voters also look evenly split by education with slightly more having high school or less education; they also tend to have lower income levels with a clear majority having $75K or less. Higher income levels tend to correlate with higher education levels. For example, almost no one making less the $40K a year has a college education, and the vast majority of earners making $125K a year or more have a college education.
